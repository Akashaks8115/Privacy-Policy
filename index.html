Varanasi Ke Chetra — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10 December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome! Varanasi Ke Chetra is a travel and information app that provides details about popular places in Varanasi, such as ghats, temples, food spots, historical locations, and local activities. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information.

2. Key Summary

  • No age restrictions — the app is suitable for all users.
  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data.
  • The app only requests Location Permission to enable certain features.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

To be clear, Varanasi Ke Chetra does not collect or store:

  • Personal identifiable information (such as name, phone number, email, etc.)
  • Login details or account information
  • User activity logs or analytics (unless added separately by you)

We do not track or save any user location on our servers.

4. Location Permission — Why We Use It

The app requests access to your device’s Location only for optional features, such as:

  • Showing nearby ghats, temples, and other places
  • Providing route suggestions (if such features are enabled)

Important: Your location is never stored, shared, uploaded, or recorded. It is used only on your device temporarily to improve your experience.

How to Disable Location Permission

You can disable location access anytime:

Settings → Apps → Varanasi Ke Chetra → Permissions → Location

5. Cookies & Local Storage

This app does not use cookies or store any personal information. If any web content is shown within the app, it may use temporary browser storage, but no personal data is collected.

6. Third-Party Content

The app may display external links, maps, or images. These external resources are governed by their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for third-party data handling.

7. No Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any user information, we do not share anything with any third party.

8. No Age Restriction

The app is available for users of all ages. It contains only tourism-related informational content.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to keep the app safe, but we cannot control the security practices of external websites or services that may appear inside the app.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if needed. Any changes will be posted on this page.
